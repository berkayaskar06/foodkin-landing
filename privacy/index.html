Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026 · Applies to the FoodKin mobile app and this website.

What we collect

  • Waitlist (this website): your email address and the channel you came from (e.g. TikTok or Instagram). Nothing else.
  • App account: email address and an optional display name.
  • App content: your pantry items, shopping lists, recipes, meal plans and household membership — the data the app exists to manage.
  • Photos: receipt and shelf photos you choose to scan are sent to our AI provider for recognition, used only to extract items, and are not retained as images.
  • Guest mode: a random device identifier stored on your device, used to keep your data together without an account.

What we do with it

  • Run the product: sync your pantry across your household, send expiry notifications, generate recipes from your ingredients.
  • Email you once when the app launches (waitlist). No marketing sequences, no spam.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising.

Where it lives

Data is stored with Supabase (hosted PostgreSQL) and protected by row-level security: your household's data is visible only to its members. AI features (recipe generation, photo recognition) are processed by our AI gateway provider; only the minimum required content is sent.

Deleting your data

  • App account: Profile → "Delete account permanently". This removes your account, profile and — if you are the household's only member — the household and all of its data. Irreversible.
  • Waitlist: reply to any email from us, or contact us below, and we'll remove your address.

Contact

Questions or requests: message @foodkinapp on Instagram or TikTok.

We'll update this page as the product evolves; material changes will be noted in the app.
